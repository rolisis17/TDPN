Onboarding lanes

Choose the path that matches your role and responsibility.

Both lanes begin with wallet sign-in. Client workflows continue immediately, while operator privileges unlock only after a deliberate application and approval sequence.

Client self-serve lane

Register quickly, then operate as a trusted client.

Built for users who need immediate onboarding with policy-aware defaults and explicit readiness signals from session creation through registration.

  1. Sign in: Complete wallet challenge and establish session.
  2. Register: Select profile and submit client onboarding details.
  3. Continue: Move directly into client-capable controls.

Operator approval lane

Apply for elevated access and unlock controls after review.

Designed for operator governance with visible pending, approved, or rejected states so elevated actions only become available when conditions are satisfied.

  1. Apply: Submit operator identity and required context.
  2. Await review: Approval posture remains visible while pending.
  3. Operate: Approved sessions unlock operator lifecycle actions.

Flow cues

Operational expectations stay visible throughout onboarding.

Client lane cues

  • Session and registration checkpoints are shown as soon as the portal opens.
  • Recommended next actions progress from challenge to verify to register.
  • Client work does not require operator approval when role conditions are met.

Operator lane cues

  • Pending, approved, and rejected application states remain explicit.
  • Lock messaging explains required conditions before elevated actions unlock.
  • Lifecycle controls activate only when approval and readiness posture align.
