Why GPM

Trust-First Bootstrap

First contact happens through a pinned trusted domain. After that, the network works from distributed bootstrap directories and signed local cache fallback.

Role-Aware Operations

Client onboarding is self-serve. Operator activation is permissioned and identity-bound, with clear state transitions and approval visibility.

Desktop + Web Consistency

The desktop and portal share one product language, making onboarding, support, and governance workflows easier to understand.

Release Direction

V1 scope includes wallet-first sign in, client registration, operator application/approval tracking, and role-gated client/server controls in desktop.
