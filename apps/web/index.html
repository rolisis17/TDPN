Mental map

The beta flow is one signed chain, checked locally.

Access recovery is deliberately narrow: establish the trusted key, inspect a signed recovery pack or invite, verify it in the browser, choose an allowed helper bridge, and preserve operator evidence. Pilot handoff still requires real helper HTTPS evidence plus a trusted verifier receipt bound to the current evidence hashes.

Helper bridge paths

A helper can get someone to the door, but cannot change the lock.

Bridge paths are designed for assisted first contact and recovery continuity. They expose enough status for users to avoid bad paths while preserving a simple recovery experience.
