# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
Participants are expected to be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Technical disagreement without personal attacks
- Clear and evidence-based feedback
- Respect for privacy and security boundaries

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or hate speech
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warning

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove or edit content and restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting
Report incidents privately to project maintainers (or repository owners). Include links/screenshots where possible.

All reports will be reviewed and handled as confidentially as possible.
